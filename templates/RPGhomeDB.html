I love litRPG!

But I get tired of protagonists who beat the odds too consistently.

I wanted to see how real probabilities translate to succes in an RPG.

You can play RPB Poker and predict the winner of two 5 man teams without signing in.

If you want to manage a team of 5 players you can create a player who will manage 3 teams of randomly generated adventurers.
